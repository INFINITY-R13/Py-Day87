# Breakout Game Instructions

## How to Play
- Use **Left/Right arrow keys** or **A/D keys** to move the paddle
- Keep the ball in play by bouncing it off your paddle
- Destroy all the colored bricks to win
- You have 3 lives - don't let the ball fall off the bottom!

## Game Features
- 5 rows of colorful bricks (50 total)
- Ball physics with paddle angle influence
- Score tracking (10 points per brick)
- Lives system
- Win/lose conditions
- Restart functionality (press 'r' after game over)

## Controls
- **Left Arrow** or **A**: Move paddle left
- **Right Arrow** or **D**: Move paddle right
- **R**: Restart game (after game over)
- **Click**: Exit game

## Tips
- Hit the ball with different parts of the paddle to control its angle
- Try to clear the top rows first for higher scores
- The ball speed increases slightly as you play

Enjoy your retro gaming experience!
